Fun Facts About Bones

  • Bones ran for 12 seasons, making it one of the longest-running crime dramas.
  • The show was inspired by the real-life forensic anthropologist Kathy Reichs.
  • Angela’s full name is “Angela Pearly-Gates Montenegro.”
  • The Gormogon story line happened because the writers were on strike
  • Emily Deschanel "Bones", didn't think the show would last more than 3 years
  • Even though the show was set in Washington it was actually filmed in LA
  • The show has a spin off called "The Finder" which only last 1 season
