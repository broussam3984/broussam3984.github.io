Bones: Show Overview

Bones aired from 2005 to 2017 with 12 seasons. The series follows Dr. Temperance Brennan, a forensic anthropologist, and FBI Agent Seeley Booth as they solve murders using evidence from human remains. The show mixes science, mystery, and humor while exploring the personal relationships of the Jeffersonian team.
